Chocolate Waffle

Ingredients: Flour, Cocoa powder, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add egg, milk, butter.
  3. Whisk into smooth batter.
  4. Cook in waffle maker.
  5. Serve hot.

Strawberry Waffle

Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter, Strawberries, Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Prepare waffle batter.
  2. Cook in waffle maker.
  3. Add strawberries and cream.
  4. Serve immediately.

Nutella Waffle

Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter, Nutella

Steps

  1. Make the batter.
  2. Cook until golden.
  3. Spread Nutella on top.
  4. Serve warm.

Banana Waffle

Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter, Bananas, Honey

Steps

  1. Prepare waffle batter.
  2. Cook in waffle maker.
  3. Add banana slices.
  4. Drizzle honey on top.
  5. Serve fresh.

Blueberry Waffle

Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter, Blueberries, Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Prepare batter.
  2. Cook waffle.
  3. Top with blueberries.
  4. Add whipped cream.
  5. Serve immediately.
